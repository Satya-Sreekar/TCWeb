CASE STUDY

Professional Services

AI outsourcing for knowledge workflows and client analytics delivering productivity and insights at a global consulting firm

Overview

A global professional services and consulting firm engaged Telcomet's AI outsourcing services to transform knowledge management and client analytics workflows. The firm's consultants spent significant time on manual research, document preparation, data analysis, and client reporting. Knowledge was siloed across practice areas, client insights were fragmented, and scaling consulting capacity required proportional headcount increases. Telcomet's dedicated AI team was deployed to automate research workflows, build intelligent knowledge systems, and create AI-powered client analytics capabilities.

Industry Challenge

Time-Intensive Research

Consultants spent 30-40% of their time on manual research and data gathering

Siloed Knowledge

Expertise and deliverables were isolated across practice areas without knowledge sharing

Fragmented Client Data

Client information was scattered across systems making holistic analysis impossible

Linear Scaling Model

Growing client base required proportional consultant headcount increases

AI-Led Transformation Approach

1

Knowledge Workflow Assessment

Analysis of research, analysis, and deliverable creation processes across practice areas

2

AI-Powered Research Assistant

Automated research gathering, synthesis, and summarization from internal and external sources

3

Intelligent Knowledge Repository

AI-organized searchable repository connecting expertise across practice areas

4

Client Analytics Platform

Unified client intelligence with AI-driven insights, trend analysis, and opportunity identification

5

Automated Report Generation

AI-assisted creation of client deliverables, proposals, and analytical reports

Final Outcome

AI outsourcing transformed the consulting firm's operations and productivity. Consultants reclaimed significant time previously spent on manual research, enabling focus on strategic advisory work. Knowledge sharing across practice areas improved dramatically through the AI-organized repository. Client insights became deeper and more actionable with unified analytics. The firm broke its linear scaling model, growing revenue per consultant through AI-augmented productivity. Research cycles that previously took weeks were completed in days, accelerating project delivery and client satisfaction.
