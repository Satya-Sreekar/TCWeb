CASE STUDY

Manufacturing Enterprise

AI-driven transformation for multi-plant operations delivering measurable improvements across manufacturing workflows

Overview

A large manufacturing enterprise engaged Telcomet to digitally transform its multi-plant operations using AI. The organization faced disconnected production systems, manual planning processes, limited operational visibility, and rising costs as production volumes increased. Telcomet introduced AI-driven transformation to unify IT and operational technology data, optimize production planning, and embed predictive intelligence across manufacturing workflows. The objective was to improve efficiency, reduce downtime, and enable scalable production growth while maintaining quality, safety, and cost control across geographically distributed facilities.

Industry Challenge

Disconnected Production Systems

Production systems across plants leading to inconsistent data and reporting

Manual Planning & Scheduling

Resulting in delays and inefficient resource utilization

Limited Operational Visibility

Poor visibility into operations, equipment performance, and supply chain risks

Scaling Challenges

Increased production meant higher labor and operational costs

AI-Led Transformation Approach

1

AI Maturity Assessment

Comprehensive assessment across IT, OT, and data infrastructure to identify high-impact AI opportunities

2

AI-Powered Process Re-engineering

Redesigned production planning, inventory, and quality control workflows with AI integration

3

System Integration

Integrated AI analytics with ERP and shop-floor systems for seamless data flow

4

Predictive Maintenance Models

Implemented machine learning models to anticipate equipment failures and optimize maintenance scheduling

5

Real-Time Performance Dashboards

AI-enabled dashboards providing real-time plant monitoring and operational insights

Additional Benefits

Ability to scale production volumes without increasing workforce size, improving margins and operational resilience

Final Outcome

AI-led transformation delivered measurable improvements across manufacturing operations. Unplanned downtime was significantly reduced through predictive maintenance, production efficiency improved, and inventory levels were optimized using intelligent forecasting. Leadership gained real-time visibility into plant performance, enabling faster corrective actions and better capacity planning. The organization successfully scaled production volumes without proportional increases in labor or overhead, improving margins and operational resilience. AI became a foundational capability supporting continuous improvement, operational stability, and long-term manufacturing excellence.
