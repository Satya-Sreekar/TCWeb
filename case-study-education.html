CASE STUDY

Education Group

AI Automation for academic and administrative operations delivering efficiency across a multi-campus institution

Overview

A multi-campus education group engaged Telcomet to automate academic and administrative operations using AI. The institution managed admissions, scheduling, grading, and student services across multiple campuses with largely manual processes. Growing enrollment strained existing systems, causing delays in admissions processing, inconsistent communication, and mounting administrative burden on faculty and staff. Telcomet deployed AI automation to streamline admissions, automate scheduling, and create intelligent student engagement workflows.

Industry Challenge

Manual Admissions Processing

Paper-based applications and manual review created weeks-long processing cycles

Fragmented Communication

Students, faculty, and staff used disconnected channels causing missed updates

Scheduling Complexity

Course scheduling across campuses was manual, error-prone, and time-consuming

Administrative Overload

Faculty spent excessive time on admin tasks rather than teaching and research

AI-Led Transformation Approach

1

Academic Workflow Assessment

Comprehensive mapping of admissions, enrollment, scheduling, and student service processes

2

AI-Powered Admissions Pipeline

Automated application screening, document verification, and eligibility scoring

3

Intelligent Scheduling Engine

AI-optimized course scheduling factoring in faculty availability, room capacity, and student preferences

4

Automated Student Communications

AI-driven notifications, reminders, and query resolution across all channels

5

Faculty Dashboard & Analytics

Centralized platform for grade management, attendance tracking, and workload monitoring

Business Impact & ROI

60%

Reduction in Admin Workload

3x

Faster Admissions Processing

85%

Student Satisfaction

40%

Improvement in Resource Utilization

Final Outcome

AI automation transformed the education group's operations across all campuses. Admissions processing time was dramatically reduced through automated screening and verification, administrative workload decreased significantly for faculty and staff, and scheduling accuracy improved with AI optimization. Students experienced faster responses and consistent communication. The institution successfully scaled to accommodate growing enrollment without proportional increases in administrative staff, while faculty redirected time toward teaching and research excellence.
