CASE STUDY

Telecommunications

AI Agents for network support, incident management, and NOC operations delivering faster resolution and lower costs

Overview

A telecommunications provider engaged Telcomet to deploy AI agents across its network operations center (NOC) and customer support functions. The organization managed a large-scale network infrastructure with thousands of daily incidents, many requiring manual triage, classification, and routing. NOC teams were overwhelmed with alerts, incident resolution was slow, and escalation paths were inconsistent. Telcomet deployed agentic AI to autonomously triage incidents, predict network issues, and optimize NOC workflows for faster resolution.

Industry Challenge

Alert Overload

NOC teams received thousands of alerts daily, many duplicates or low-priority, causing fatigue

Slow Incident Triage

Manual classification and routing of incidents delayed resolution by hours

Inconsistent Escalation

Lack of standardized escalation paths led to missed SLAs and customer impact

Reactive Network Management

Network issues were detected only after customer complaints or service degradation

AI-Led Transformation Approach

1

NOC Workflow Analysis

Comprehensive mapping of incident lifecycle from detection to resolution

2

AI-Powered Alert Correlation

Intelligent deduplication and correlation of network alerts to reduce noise

3

Autonomous Incident Triage

AI agents classifying, prioritizing, and routing incidents based on impact and urgency

4

Predictive Network Monitoring

Machine learning models identifying potential failures before they cause outages

5

Automated Resolution Playbooks

AI agents executing standard resolution procedures for known incident types

Business Impact & ROI

55%

Faster Incident Triage

45%

Reduction in NOC Costs

70%

Reduction in Alert Noise

35%

Improvement in SLA Compliance

Final Outcome

AI agents transformed the telecom provider's network operations. Incident triage time was cut dramatically through autonomous classification and routing, NOC costs decreased as AI agents handled routine incidents independently, and alert noise was significantly reduced through intelligent correlation. Predictive monitoring enabled proactive issue resolution before customer impact. SLA compliance improved markedly as incidents were resolved faster and escalated more consistently. The provider scaled network operations to support infrastructure growth without proportional increases in NOC staffing.
